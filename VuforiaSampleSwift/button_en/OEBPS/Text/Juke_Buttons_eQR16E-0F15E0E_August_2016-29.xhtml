AIR INTAKE CONTROL LEVER

MANUAL AIR CONDITIONER

When the air intake control lever is set to the right, interior air is recirculated in the interior. If the lever is set to the left, outside air will enter the interior.

Air recirculation may be desired to avoid unpleasant smells, exhaust fumes or dust being drawn into the interior. Under normal conditions, fresh air intake mode should be selected to avoid the air in the interior becoming stale and windows fogging up.
