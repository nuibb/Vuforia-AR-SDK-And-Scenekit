DRIVE INFO BUTTON

Within the Drive mode, various types of drive information can be displayed.

  • Press the <D-MODE> button to enter the Drive mode.
  • Press the <DRIVE INFO> button.
    The display will show the elapsed time, average speed and distance driven since the last reset.
    To reset the data, press the <PUSH ENTER> dial for approx. 1 second.
  • Press the <DRIVE INFO> button again.
    A G-force meter will be displayed, indicating the G-forces acting horizontally and laterally on the vehicle during acceleration, braking and cornering.
