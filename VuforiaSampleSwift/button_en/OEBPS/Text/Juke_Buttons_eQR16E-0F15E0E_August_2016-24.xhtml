DAY/NIGHT / SETUP BUTTON

COMBINED AUDIO AND NAVIGATION SYSTEM (“NISSAN CONNECT”)

button

Press to change the display brightness between day and night modes. While the mode is being displayed, the brightness can be adjusted using the zoom/scroll dial.

Press and hold the button for more than two seconds to switch the display to show a larger display of the digital clock.

<SETUP> button

Press to access the [SETUP] menu in which you can configure settings for:

  • Audio
  • Navigation
  • Telephone and Bluetooth
  • System
  • Traffic Messages
  • Radio
