TELEPHONE MENU BUTTON

AUDIO SYSTEM (WITHOUT NAVIGATION)

Press the button to enter the phone menu from which you can select one of the following actions:

  • Scan for a device: the audio unit searches Bluetooth devices and shows all visible devices. Press the <ENTER> button to select and pair a device.
  • Pair device: a maximum of five Bluetooth devices can be paired with the system.
  • Select a device: paired Bluetooth devices are listed and one can be selected for connection.
  • Delete a device: a registered Bluetooth device can be deleted.
  • Bluetooth: to switch Bluetooth signal on or off.
