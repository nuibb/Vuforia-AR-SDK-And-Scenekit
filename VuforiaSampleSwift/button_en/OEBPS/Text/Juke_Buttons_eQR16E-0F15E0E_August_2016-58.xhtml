DRIVE MODE CONTROL BUTTON

Press the <D-MODE> button to enter the drive mode and select one of the available drive modes. After a selection is made, the control states of the affected systems will be briefly displayed.

Note: The specific control states being displayed depend on the specification of the vehicle.
