SETUP MENU

AUDIO SYSTEM (WITHOUT NAVIGATION)

Use the <SETUP> button to configure [Audio], [Clock], [Radio], and [Language] settings as follows:

1. Press the <SETUP> button.

2. Turn the <TUNE/MENU> dial clockwise or anticlockwise; the display will appear in the following order: [Audio]  [Clock]  [Radio]  [Language].

3. Press the <ENTER> button to enter a menu and make settings using the <TUNE/MENU> dial and the <ENTER> button.

After the desired settings have been made, press either the <BACK> button repeatedly, the <SETUP> button or wait for 8 seconds without pressing any buttons to exit the menu screen.
