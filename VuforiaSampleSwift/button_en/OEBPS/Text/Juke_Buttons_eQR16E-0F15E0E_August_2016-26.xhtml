ZOOM/SCROLL DIAL AND ENTER BUTTON

COMBINED AUDIO AND NAVIGATION SYSTEM (“NISSAN CONNECT”)

  • In Navigation mode, turn the dial to zoom in/out on a map.
  • In Menu mode, turn the dial to scroll through the options displayed in a menu. Press the <ENTER> button to confirm a selection.
  • In Radio mode, turn the dial to manually change the frequency.
