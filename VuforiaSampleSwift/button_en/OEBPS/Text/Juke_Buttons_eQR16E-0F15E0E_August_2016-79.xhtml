DRIVE MODE [NORMAL] BUTTON

In this drive mode, the balance between vehicle performance and fuel economy is optimised.

Press the <NORMAL> button to select this drive mode. The display will show you the actual situation on instantaneous battery charging* and engine torque delivered.

* The battery charging system is designed only to charge the battery when there is a low demand on the engine. Thus, the meter will not always show the maximum reading. This is not an indication of battery health.
