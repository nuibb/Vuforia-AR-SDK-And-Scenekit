OPEN AIR ROOF

Opening/Closing and Tilting

The open air roof only operates with the ignition switched ON.

To open or close the open air roof partially, press and hold the open air roof switch to OPEN or CLOSE position and release it.

To fully open or close the open air roof automatically, shortly press the switch fully to the OPEN or CLOSE position.

To tilt the rear of the open air roof, press the centre of the switch

Auto-reverse function

If the open air roof system detects an object being caught during closing, the system will automatically reverse the movement and open the roof
