AUTO STOP/START SYSTEM OFF SWITCH

The Auto Stop/Start system automatically stops the engine when at idle and will automatically restart the engine. If the system is activated, the Auto Stop/Start symbol is shown in the vehicle information display.

To temporarily switch of the Auto Stop/Start system, press the Auto Stop/Start system OFF switch.

Turn on the Auto Stop/Start system by pressing the Stop/Start system OFF switch again. The Auto Stop/Start system is also automatically turned on again once the engine is stopped and restarted.
