SEEK/PREVIOUS TRACK/NEXT TRACK AND FAST REVERSE (REVIEW)/FAST FORWARD (CUE) BUTTONS

COMBINED AUDIO AND NAVIGATION SYSTEM (“NISSAN CONNECT”)

FM radio mode:

Seek button

Pressing the button starts the tuning mode. The radio tuner seeks from high to low/low to high frequencies and stops at the previous/next broadcasting station. During seek mode, the audio output is muted. If no broadcasting station can be found within the complete band cycle, it will return to the initial frequency.

DAB (Digital Radio) mode

Pressing the button will select the previous/next station.

CD and AUX mode:

Previous Track/Next Track and Fast Reverse (Review) / Fast Forward (Cue) button

When the button is pressed shortly, the previous/next track from a CD or externally connected device will be played.

When the button is pressed continuously, the track will be played (reverse or forward) at high speed. When the button is released, the track will be played at normal playing speed again.
