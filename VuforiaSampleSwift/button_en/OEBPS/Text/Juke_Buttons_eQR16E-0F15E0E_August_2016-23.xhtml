NAV/INFO BUTTON

COMBINED AUDIO AND NAVIGATION SYSTEM (“NISSAN CONNECT”)

NAV/INFO button

Press <NAV> to access the functions of the Navigation system.

Press <INFO> to access the [INFO] screen, in which you can choose between following options:

  • [My Apps] Selecting [My Apps] will show you the selection of available in-vehicle applications (apps). To have access to My Apps, the Nissan Connect App must be running on your Smartphone, connected via Bluetooth® or USB.
  • [Traffic Messages] Selecting [Traffic Messages] allows you to influence the route as calculated by the navigation system, based on current traffic conditions.
  • [ECO score] Selecting [ECO Score] provides you with details on the economy-level of your drive style since the last reset.
