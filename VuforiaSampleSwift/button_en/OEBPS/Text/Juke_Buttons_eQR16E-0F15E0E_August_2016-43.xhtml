REAR WINDOW DEFOGGER BUTTON

AUTOMATIC AIR CONDITIONER

To defog/defrost the rear window, push the rear window defogger button. The indicator light in the button will illuminate. The defogger will turn off automatically after approximately 5 minutes. If the rear window clears before this time, push the button again to manually turn the defogger off.

When the rear window defogger is activated, the outside door mirrors will be defogged as well.
