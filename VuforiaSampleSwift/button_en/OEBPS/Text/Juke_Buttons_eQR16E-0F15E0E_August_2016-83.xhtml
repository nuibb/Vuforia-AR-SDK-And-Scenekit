ECO INFO BUTTON

Within the Drive mode, different fuel economy records can be displayed.

  • Press the <D-MODE> button to enter the Drive mode.
  • Press the <ECO INFO> button repeatedly to display fuel economy records as follows:
    • Daily Reset – displays the fuel economy history gathered over the past 5 days.
    • Weekly Reset – displays the fuel economy history gathered over the past 5 weeks.
    • Reset at Start – displays the fuel economy history gathered since the last 5 times the ignition was switched on.
    • Manual Reset – displays the fuel economy history gathered since the last 5 times that the system was manually reset.
      To manually reset the fuel economy history data, press the <PUSH ENTER> dial for more than approx. 1 second to clear each of the fuel economy histograms.
