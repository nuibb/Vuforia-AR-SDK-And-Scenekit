RES/+ AND SET/- SWITCH

CRUISE CONTROL AND SPEED LIMITER SWITCHES

  • To resume cruise control or speed limiter operation after cancellation, press <RES/+> switch.
  • To increase set cruising speed or speed limit, press <RES/+> switch.
  • To set cruising speed or speed limit, press <SET/-> switch.
  • To decrease set cruising speed or speed limit, press <SET/-> switch.
