SETUP MODE BUTTON

Within the Drive mode, you can adjust various settings and functions to your preferences.

  • Press the <D-MODE> button to enter the Drive mode.
  • Press the <SETUP> button.
  • Turn the <PUSH ENTER> dial to scroll through the list of available functions that can be adjusted, like display and button brightness selection, language and unit selection, clock setting, etc.
    Refer to your vehicle's Owner's Manual for full details.

    Note: Adjustments in the setup mode cannot be made while driving. The display will be obscured to indicate this.

DISPLAY BRIGHTNESS CONTROL

A long press on the <SETUP> button switches the display brightness manually between day and night modes.
